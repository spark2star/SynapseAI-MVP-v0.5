COLORS:
- Background: White with slight transparency
- Duration: Red text (#dc2626) on pink background
- Manual indicator: Blue text (#2563eb)
- Send button: Blue background (#2563eb), white text
- Send button hover: Darker blue (#1d4ed8)
- Send button disabled: Gray (#9ca3af)
- Status: Gray text on light gray background

POSITIONING:
- Fixed to bottom-right corner
- 20px from right edge
- 20px from bottom edge
- Floats above all other content (z-index: 1000)
- Shadow for depth
- Rounded corners (12px)

BEHAVIOR:
- Only visible when recording is active
- Disappears when recording stops
- Duration updates every 1 second
- Manual mode toggle works instantly
- Send button disabled when:
  * Not recording
  * Processing in progress
  * No audio data collected yet
